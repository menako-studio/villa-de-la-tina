## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW & OBJECTIVES

### 1.1 Background
Villa De Latina Kuningan requires a professional website to serve as a digital platform showcasing community events, political portfolio, and media coverage of Hj. Tina Wiryawati, S.H - a member of DPRD Jawa Barat representing Dapil 13 (Kuningan, Ciamis, Banjar, Pangandaran).

### 1.2 Design Objectives
- Create a professional, trustworthy, and modern website design that reflects political credibility
- Develop user-friendly interfaces optimized for desktop and mobile devices
- Establish a strong visual identity for Villa De Latina Kuningan as a community event hub
- Showcase past events and upcoming initiatives in an engaging manner
- Integrate media coverage and social media seamlessly
- Design for future scalability and content updates

### 1.3 Success Metrics
- Professional aesthetic that enhances political brand perception
- Intuitive navigation with maximum 3 clicks to reach any content
- Mobile-responsive design meeting modern UX standards
- Clear visual hierarchy prioritizing key information
- Client approval within 2 revision rounds

## 6. VISUAL DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

### 6.1 Brand Alignment
**Villa De Latina Kuningan Identity**:
- Professional, trustworthy, and modern
- Colors should reflect stability, growth, and community
- Balance between political professionalism and warmth

**Suggested Color Palette** (to be finalized with client):
- Primary: Deep blue or navy (credibility, trust, stability)
- Secondary: Gold or warm orange (energy, optimism, warmth)
- Accent: Green (growth, community, harmony)
- Neutrals: Clean whites, soft grays for backgrounds

### 6.2 Typography
- **Headings**: Professional sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Poppins, Montserrat)
- **Body Text**: Highly readable font (e.g., Open Sans, Roboto, Source Sans)
- **Hierarchy**: Clear sizing differences (H1, H2, H3, body, captions)
- **Accessibility**: Minimum 16px for body text, sufficient contrast ratios

### 6.3 Visual Style Guidelines
- **Photography Style**: Professional, authentic, community-focused
- **Iconography**: Simple, modern, consistent style
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace for clarity and focus
- **Buttons/CTAs**: Clear, prominent, with hover states
- **Cards**: Subtle shadows, rounded corners (modern feel)

### 6.4 Responsive Design Strategy
**Breakpoints**:
- Mobile: 375px - 767px
- Tablet: 768px - 1023px
- Desktop: 1024px+

**Mobile-First Approach**:
- Design mobile layouts first
- Progressive enhancement for larger screens
- Touch-friendly buttons (minimum 44x44px)
- Simplified navigation for mobile

## 10. REVISION PROCESS

### 10.1 Revision Policy
**Included in Budget**: 2 rounds of revisions

### 10.2 Revision Scope
**What's Included**:
- Color adjustments
- Typography changes
- Layout modifications
- Content reorganization
- Component styling tweaks
- Copy/text updates
- Image replacements

**What's NOT Included** (Additional scope):
- Adding new screens beyond 5 screens
- Complete redesign of approved elements
- New features not in original scope
- Animated graphics/videos
- Custom illustrations

### 10.3 Revision Process Flow
1. **Client submits feedback** via shared document (Google Docs/Figma comments)
2. **Designer reviews** and clarifies requirements if needed (1 day)
3. **Designer implements** revisions (2-3 days per round)
4. **Client reviews** updated designs
5. **Approval or second round** (if needed)

### 10.4 Feedback Guidelines
**Effective Feedback**:
- Be specific (not "make it pop", but "increase contrast on CTA buttons")
- Reference specific screens/components
- Explain the "why" behind requested changes
- Consolidate all feedback per review round
- Provide feedback within 2 business days of presentation
