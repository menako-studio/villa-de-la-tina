## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW & OBJECTIVES

### 1.1 Background
The Villa De Latina Kuningan website development project requires comprehensive project management to coordinate multiple teams (UI/UX Designer, Developer, SEO Specialist, Copywriter) and ensure successful, on-time, on-budget delivery of a professional website for Hj. Tina Wiryawati, S.H.

### 1.2 Project Management Objectives
- **Coordination**: Seamlessly coordinate 4+ team members and client stakeholders
- **Timeline Management**: Deliver project within 12-week timeline
- **Budget Management**: Ensure project stays within Rp 24,000,000 budget
- **Quality Assurance**: Maintain high standards across all deliverables
- **Risk Management**: Identify and mitigate project risks proactively
- **Communication**: Facilitate clear, timely communication between all parties
- **Client Satisfaction**: Ensure client needs are met and expectations exceeded

### 1.3 Success Metrics
- Project completed within 12-week timeline (±1 week tolerance)
- Budget adherence (no cost overruns)
- All deliverables meet acceptance criteria
- Client satisfaction score: 4.5/5 or higher
- Zero critical issues at launch
- Smooth handoff and knowledge transfer

## 8. CHANGE MANAGEMENT

### 8.1 Change Request Process

**When is a change request needed?**
- Adding new pages/features not in original scope
- Significant design changes after approval
- Timeline extensions beyond ±1 week
- Budget increases
- Changes to core functionality

**Change Request Workflow**:
1. **Request Submission**: Stakeholder submits change request
2. **Impact Analysis**: PM analyzes impact on scope, time, cost
3. **Recommendation**: PM provides recommendation
4. **Client Approval**: Client approves or rejects with signature
5. **Implementation**: If approved, change incorporated into plan
6. **Documentation**: Change logged in change log

**Change Request Form Includes**:
- Description of change
- Reason for change
- Impact on scope
- Impact on timeline
- Impact on budget
- Recommendation
- Approval signatures

## 11. RISK MANAGEMENT PLAN

### 11.1 Top Project Risks & Mitigation

**Risk 1: Client Approval Delays**
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Impact**: High (delays entire timeline)
- **Mitigation**: 
  - Set clear expectation: 2 business days for feedback
  - Send reminders if deadline approaching
  - Have backup decision maker identified

**Risk 2: Content Not Ready on Time**
- **Probability**: High
- **Impact**: Medium-High
- **Mitigation**:
  - Provide content templates early
  - Set hard deadlines with buffer
  - Have placeholder content ready if needed

**Risk 3: Design Revisions Exceed 2 Rounds**
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Impact**: Medium (timeline and cost)
- **Mitigation**:
  - Detailed design brief upfront
  - Regular check-ins during design
  - Clear revision scope definition

**Risk 4: Technical Issues During Development**
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Impact**: High
- **Mitigation**:
  - Developer provides technical feasibility review early
  - Buffer time in schedule (10% contingency)
  - Regular development demos to catch issues early

**Risk 5: Launch Day Technical Issues**
- **Probability**: Low
- **Impact**: High
- **Mitigation**:
  - Comprehensive pre-launch testing
  - Launch on low-traffic day/time
  - Have developer on standby during launch
  - Backup of old site (if applicable)
